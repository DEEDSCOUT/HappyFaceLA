Safety and trust notes

  • Happy Faces LA uses professional cosmetic-grade face paints, cosmetic glitter, skin-safe temporary tattoos, and face/body-safe adhesives for gems and face jewelry. We do not use craft glitter or craft glue on the skin.
  • For hygiene, supplies are kept clean during setup and throughout the event. Products are not applied to broken, irritated, sunburned, or visibly contagious skin. Single-use applicators are used where appropriate.
  • Guests with sensitive skin, allergies, skin conditions, or recent reactions should let us know before service so designs can be adjusted or skipped when needed.
  • Insured through Body Art Insurance. COI availability is confirmed during booking.
  • We serve Los Angeles County, Orange County, and Ventura County. Setup details are confirmed at booking.

Frequently asked questions

How long does face painting take per child?

Most designs take 3–7 minutes per child depending on complexity. Simple cheek art is faster; full-face designs take longer. Happy Faces LA focuses on fast line management so more guests get painted in less time.

What face painting designs are available?

Designs include butterflies, superheroes, animals, fantasy characters, sports themes, and simple cheek art. Guests choose from a design menu at the event. Custom theme requests can be discussed during booking.

Is face painting safe for toddlers and younger children?

Happy Faces LA uses professional cosmetic-grade face paints formulated for skin use. Product is not applied to broken, irritated, or sensitive skin. For very young children or guests with known skin conditions, parents can request that designs be skipped or adjusted.

How do children remove face paint after the party?

Most cosmetic-grade face paint washes off with warm water and gentle soap or a damp cloth. Most designs come off within one or two gentle washes. Avoid scrubbing.

What is the minimum booking for face painting?

Bookings start at $150. Final pricing depends on event date, location, guest count, and event length. Submit your event details for an accurate quote.
