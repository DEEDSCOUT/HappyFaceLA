## 3. Official Policy Sources (HFLA-EVD-004 through HFLA-EVD-005)

### HFLA-EVD-004 — Google Ads Misrepresentation Policy
- **URL:** https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/6020955
- **Access date:** 2025-07-21
- **Key facts captured:**
  - Prohibits omitting or obscuring billing details (how, what, when users will be charged)
  - Prohibits making offers that aren't actually available
  - Prohibits misleading or unrealistic claims
  - Ads must be clear, honest, provide information users need to make informed decisions
  - Price claims in ads must match the landing page
- **Reliability:** Tier 1 (official Google policy documentation)
- **Rules supported:** HFLA-RULE-CI-001

### HFLA-EVD-005 — Google Ads Editorial and Destination Requirements
- **URL:** https://support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/6008942
- **Access date:** 2025-07-21
- **Key facts captured:**
  - Editorial: prohibits overly generic ads with vague phrases
  - Destination requirements: landing pages must offer unique value, be functional and easy to navigate
  - Display URLs must accurately reflect the landing page URL
  - Ad format requirements: character limits apply to headlines and descriptions
  - Child/teen protection policies apply (relevant for HFLA's family-audience targeting)
- **Reliability:** Tier 1 (official Google policy documentation)
- **Rules supported:** HFLA-RULE-CI-001, HFLA-RULE-AR-001
